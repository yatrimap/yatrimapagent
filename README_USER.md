## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### "I see a blank page"
- Wait a few seconds (might be loading)
- Refresh the page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
- Clear browser cache (see browser settings)
- Log out and log back in

### "Data not updating"
- Click the refresh button on the page
- The system refreshes automatically every 30 seconds
- Try logging out and back in

### "Withdrawal failed"
- Check you have at least ₹500 available
- Check your bank details in profile
- Try again in a few moments
- Contact support if it continues

### "Pooling opportunities not showing"
- Check if your city matches available activities
- Refresh the page (pooling updates every 30 seconds)
- Check the urgency filters
- All opportunities will appear automatically
